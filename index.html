Investment Thesis

Two ways AI creates value

I — Product Edge

AI as the
core advantage

Melody takes outsized positions in companies where AI is the central product differentiator — not a feature, but the foundation.

We are hands-on, operator-led investors. We help build, scale, and support these businesses closely, drawing on direct experience founding and running AI-native companies.

II — Operating Edge

AI as the
turnaround lever

Melody also backs businesses where AI can create significant operational leverage — including distressed, underperforming, or transformation situations.

The strategy: deploy AI, automation, and hands-on operating support to improve efficiency and return businesses to profitability. This draws directly on the Jain Family Office's 20-year track record in operational turnarounds and distressed situations.

About

The Jain Family Office

Melody is the tech and venture arm of the Jain Family Office — a private investment platform with a long track record in operating and investing across businesses at different stages of growth and transformation.

The Jain Family Office has completed 15+ transactions over 20 years, with particular depth in operational turnarounds, distressed buyouts, and value creation through active ownership.

Melody draws on this heritage — combining the family office's patient capital and operational experience with a conviction-led approach to AI investment.
